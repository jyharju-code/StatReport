# Quarterly Sales Review — Example

## Executive summary

This quarter's results are summarised below for the leadership team. The narrative is
deliberately concise: each paragraph states the headline figure, the direction of travel,
and one sentence of interpretation. Tables and charts carry the detail.

## Data overview

We describe the dataset before drawing conclusions: the period covered, the number of
observations, and any gaps in the data that affect interpretation.

## Sales trend over time

We track total revenue across the period and call out the net change from the first to
the last point, noting any seasonality.

## Performance by region

Regions are compared on mean revenue. We name the strongest and weakest performers and
avoid over-interpreting small differences.

## Key drivers

Where the data supports it, we look at the relationship between units, price, discount,
and revenue to understand what moves the top line.

## Outlook

A short, measured forward look — clearly labelled as an estimate, never presented as fact.
